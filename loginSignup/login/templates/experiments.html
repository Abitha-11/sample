DNA Extraction from Fruits

DNA Extraction from Fruits

This experiment involves extracting DNA from fruits like strawberries, bananas, or kiwis using common household items such as dish soap, salt, and alcohol.

Steps:

  • Prepare the fruit sample and mash it to release juices.
  • Add detergent and salt to break down cell membranes and release DNA.
  • Incorporate alcohol to precipitate the DNA.
  • Collect the DNA using a fine filter or cheesecloth.
Microbial Growth on Agar Plates

Microbial Growth on Agar Plates

This experiment focuses on observing the growth of microorganisms like bacteria or fungi on nutrient agar plates under various conditions.

Steps:

  • Prepare nutrient agar plates and sterilize them.
  • Spread the microbial sample on the agar surface.
  • Incubate the plates at suitable temperatures for observation.
  • Document the colony morphology and characteristics.
